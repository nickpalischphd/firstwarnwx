About the Meteorologist

Meet Dr. Nick

Meteorologist PhD — Higher Education Leadership Executive Juris Doctorate NWS WRN Ambassador Public Safety Professional Author Ordained Minister

Dr. Nick is a meteorologist and public safety professional dedicated to providing clear, accurate, and timely weather information to communities across Missouri and Southern Illinois.


As an NWS Weather-Ready Nation Ambassador, Dr. Nick is committed to building weather-ready communities — helping families and businesses prepare before severe weather strikes. Holding a PhD in Higher Education Leadership and an Executive Juris Doctorate, he brings expertise, credibility, and genuine care to every forecast.


When he's not forecasting, Dr. Nick writes for Dr. Nick's Matter of Perspective, serves as an ordained minister, and spends time with his four Corgis — Willow, Mogwai, Winston, and Baxter.
